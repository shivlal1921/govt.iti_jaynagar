ELECTRICIAN

Preface We are extremely pleased to publish the first edition of the presented "Electrician Trade". This is written in the national language Hindi for the trainees of ITI Electrician Trade in Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS) under National Council of Vocational Training (NCVT). This has been prepared according to the latest curriculum NSQF Level-5 approved by Directorate General of Training (DGT). This is very useful for CTS in Electrician Trade as well as Apprenticeship Training Scheme, Craftsman Instructor Training Scheme (CITS)/CTI, and other equivalent trades like Wireman, Electroplater, Lift and Accelerator Mechanic etc. This can prove to be very useful for preparing for various competitive examinations for jobs in Electrician Trade. Every effort has been made to make the language style of the pure, simple, beautiful and fluent. At necessary places, along with Hindi words, their English words have also been used in brackets. An attempt has been made to make the subject more simple and interesting by using practical words in the. Simple and realistic pictures have been given at appropriate places to understand the entire subject matter of the well. All the information mentioned in the pictures has been given in both Hindi and English medium. To understand any trade of vocational training in depth, in-depth study is required. The presented has been prepared for the benefit of electrician trainees keeping in mind the need and level of theoretical knowledge. As per the syllabus, the chapters have been arranged in the proper order within two years. Each chapter contains technical information. Every effort has been made to explain the terms with their definitions, formulas, units and mathematical analysis as per need, so that the trainees can understand the fact deeply. Keeping in mind the modern examination systems, even the subtle topics have been explained appropriately in this . Maximum number of multiple choice questions have been included in each chapter and as per need, the selected questions have also been analyzed. Thus, keeping in view the importance of the subject, the presented has been made completely useful for the trainees. From the writing of this to its publication, our close associates have contributed a lot. They have provided us with the opportunity to bring this to you by giving us necessary suggestions from time to time. We are grateful to all of them. We express who trusted us and gave us a golden opportunity to write this . Along with this, we also thank all the employees of government who have worked hard in a short time to make the editing of this a success. If any error remains in the even after taking special care, then the readers must write to us and send them. We will be very happy to rectify the error. In the end, we request all the readers and critics to oblige us by sending their valuable suggestions regarding this , so that this can be made more useful in future.
